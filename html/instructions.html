How to Play

The quiz consists of 20 questions, one relating to each team in the Premier League this season, and each question will have four answers, one which is correct and three that are incorrect.
All questions will appear in a random order and you will get one try at each question. You can score a 'goal' by answering 4 questions correctly, every four correct answers will be another goal, you will concede a goal for every four incorrect answers. The result will be shown after clicking the 'Full Time' button after you have answered all 20 questions.
There is a 'Leave Match' button at the bottom of each page which will take you back to the home screen, your answers will not be saved if you click this button and you will have to start the quiz again.

It's you against the Computer! How will you get on?
